Multi-source Consolidation
Eliminate the $7M duplicate problem — permanently.
MDM ingests records from Salesforce, Sage Intacct, Workday, Bullhorn, and the homegrown quoting tool simultaneously. Survivorship rules decide which field value wins. No source system needs to change. The result: one master customer ID across all 9 acquired entities — the foundation for clean PE due diligence.
Reference Data Management
Board reports that don't require a week of prep.
Every BU can keep using the location names they're comfortable with. MDM maps them all to one canonical record behind the scenes. "Beaumont," "BMT," "BTR," "BYT," and "Baytown" all resolve to the same place. Tableau and CRMA pull clean data — investment bankers no longer required to manually reformat board decks.
B2B Hierarchy Management
Mac's "data texture" — delivered into Salesforce.
Marathon parent company → 9 individual sites → individual projects — all mapped in a full relationship graph and pushed to Salesforce. Total wallet share in one click. Zero-revenue sites surface automatically as cross-sell targets. Account intelligence lives in the system, not in any one person's head.
Data Governance
The guardrails Timothy asked for, built in.
When a rep starts typing a customer name, MDM suggests the existing record before a duplicate can be created. Data quality enforced at the point of entry — not corrected on the back end. Standardized KPI definitions mean the CRO, CFO, and board all see the same numbers from the same source.
